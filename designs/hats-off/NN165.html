The Needle's Notion



Hats Off
Show your patriotism by stitching a
classic Uncle Sam's hat in two ways.
The framed design is worked on 32 count
Belfast Raw Linen using Sampler Threads
and DMC floss.
The little soft sculpture hat, stitched
on 2-inch wide Aida banding, may be
used as an ornament or pin cushion.


Design size: 9 x 6 3/4 inches
