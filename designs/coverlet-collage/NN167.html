The Needle's Notion



Coverlet Collage

This striking sampler includes motifs
inspired by a variety of antique woven
coverlets. The design is stitched in
rusty red and deep navy blue floss on
32 count Sandstone/Tea Dyed Linen-Silk.
The border design includes both cross
stitch and vertical satin stitch to
give the appearance of weaving.

Design size: 8 1/2 x 11 3/4 inches
