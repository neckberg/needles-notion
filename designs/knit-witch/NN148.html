The Needle's Notion




Knit Witch

This industrious witch knits with her fiber of choice -
thread from a spider web. Her helpful spider hangs out
nearby. Stitched with hand-dyed threads from The Dye Is Cast
and DMC floss on 28 count Confederate Grey Cashel Linen.
Webs are stitched with black Kreinik braid. Knit Witch
stitches up quickly!
Stitch count: 77 wide x 88 high.
