Jack Patch
With map in hand, Jack Patch the
Pirate Pumpkin gleefully examines
his discovery - a treasure chest
filled with candy. What a find
for Halloween!

Stitched with Kreinik metallic braid,
Weeks Dye Works and DMC floss
on 28 count Thyme Hand Dyed Linen
from Wichelt. The design is
embellished with a blackbird and
and candy buttons from Just
Another Button Company.
Design size: 8.5 x 6.75 inches
Retail price: $7.00
