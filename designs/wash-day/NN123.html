Wash Day

Sunshine, birds, flowers, soap bubbles and the
companionship of a ginger cat make wash day fun!

The Wash Day band sampler is worked on 32 count
Platinum Belfast Linen with DMC floss.

Design size: 5 ½ x 15 inches.

Wash Day is the first design in
The Needle's Notion's popular days of
the week series.













The Days of the Week sampler series includes:
Wash Day , Baking Day , Cleaning Day, Market Day, Ironing Day, Sewing Day, Day of Rest
