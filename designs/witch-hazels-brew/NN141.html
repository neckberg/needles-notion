Witch Hazel's Brew
Stitch Witch Hazel and her bubbling brew as
a 14x16 inch teapot-shaped pillow or as a
framed piece. Pillow pattern and complete
instructions are included. Pillow is stitched
on 10 count White Birch Heatherfield using
DMC floss, Kreinik metallic threads and Mill
Hill Beads. Smaller framed design is worked on
26 count Heatherfield.
